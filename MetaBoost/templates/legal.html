Privacy and Security

At MetaBoost, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your data is used responsibly. Our privacy and security policies are designed to provide transparency and give you peace of mind when using our services. Below are the key principles we adhere to:

  • Data Encryption: All data processed through MetaBoost is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. This means that your information is protected both in transit and at rest, ensuring it is accessible only to you and our authorized systems.
  • Limited Data Collection: We only collect the minimum amount of personal information necessary to provide you with our services. MetaBoost does not track your activity outside of our platform, and we never share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • User Anonymity: Wherever possible, we provide options for users to use our services anonymously. Your website’s content and meta tags are analyzed without requiring personal identification details.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You have full control over your information and can request its deletion at any time.
  • Access Control and Authentication: We employ robust access control measures to ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data. Our systems use multi-factor authentication and secure password policies to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Compliance with Regulations: MetaBoost complies with global data privacy regulations, including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act). We continually update our policies and practices to meet evolving legal standards and to better protect your data.
  • Incident Response: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have a comprehensive incident response plan to quickly address the issue, notify affected users, and mitigate any potential damage.

By using MetaBoost, you agree to our Privacy Policy, which is designed to safeguard your data and ensure that your experience is secure. If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your information, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
